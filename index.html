Course Reflection

In this class, I learned everything I need to know about how to make a basic website. I feel as if I have all the tools that are necessary to not only make such a website (like the one you are looking at now), but also to keep learning. By this I mean, if in the future I want to take my web development skills to the next level, I have the fundamental knowledge that provides a foundation for me to continue forward with more complex topics on this subject. Learning HTML, CSS, and Javascript really is empowering, as I feel that now if I ever want to make a website for WHATEVER reason, I have the skills to do so, and won't have to higher outside help. I think my favorite part about this class is that, in most of the assignments, there was a bit of room for creativity. With that space for creativity, I realized each time that I had skills to make something special, and really enjoyed looking back at the finished product I'd created. I plan on making my own website soon after this course- a task that would seem much more daunting had I not picked up the fundamentals that were taught in this class.
